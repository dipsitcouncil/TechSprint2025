Cyber Quest

About Cyber Quest

Cyber Quest challenges your analytical and cybersecurity skills in a race against the clock. Participants will tackle digital puzzles, network challenges, and penetration testing scenarios to find hidden flags and vulnerabilities. This event is designed for tech enthusiasts eager to test their cybersecurity knowledge and critical thinking under pressure.

Event Details

  • Date: 12th August 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Venue: Computer Lab, DPS Bangalore South
  • Team Size: 2-3 participants per team
