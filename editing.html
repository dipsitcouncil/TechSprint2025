About Pixel Motions

Pixel Motions is a short film and animation competition designed to showcase your creativity and storytelling skills. Participants will produce a short video (animation or live-action) on a provided theme within a time limit, focusing on creativity, editing, sound, and effective message delivery. This event is perfect for students passionate about filmmaking and digital storytelling.

Event Details

  • Date: 13th August 2025
  • Time: 11:00 AM - 2:00 PM
  • Venue: AV Room, DPS Bangalore South
  • Team Size: 1-3 participants per team
Register Now

Rules

  • The theme will be disclosed on the day of the event.
  • Duration of the video should not exceed 3 minutes.
  • Participants must bring their own recording devices if needed.
  • Use of copyrighted music is not allowed unless proof of license is provided.
  • Judgment criteria: creativity, relevance to theme, technical quality, and overall impact.
