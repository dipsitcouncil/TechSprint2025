About Valorant Tournament

Join us for an electrifying Valorant tournament at Tech Sprint 2025! Teams will face off in intense tactical shooter matches, testing their aim, strategy, and teamwork under pressure. Whether you're a Duelist, Initiator, or Sentinel main, Valorant at Tech Sprint is your chance to showcase your clutch plays, outsmart your opponents, and claim victory for your team. Get ready to spike the site and defuse the competition!

Event Details

  • Date: 15th August 2025
  • Time: 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM
  • Venue: Computer Lab, DPS Bangalore South
  • Team Size: 5 players per team
