About Tech Sprint

DPS Bangalore South proudly presents Tech Sprint 2025, the ultimate celebration of technology, creativity, and future-forward thinking. Designed to ignite young minds and showcase student innovation, this dynamic tech fest features a wide range of events — from coding challenges to Gaming, Video Editing and more. Open to students from Classes 9 to 12, Tech Sprint 2025 is more than just a competition — it's a platform to explore, experiment, and excel in the world of technology. Whether you're a budding coder, a design enthusiast, a tech tinkerer, or just curious about the digital world, there’s something for everyone. Get ready to ideate, build, and sprint towards the future — because at Tech Sprint 2025, the next big idea could be yours!
